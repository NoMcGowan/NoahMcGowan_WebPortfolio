About Me

I am passionate about cybersecurity and data analysis, with the goal of ensuring safer digital experiences for all users, particularly those who may be less familiar with technology.

My journey into the tech world started with a fascination for how technology could solve real-world problems. This led me to pursue a degree in Computer Information Systems, where I've gained skills in web development, network security, and data management. Outside of my studies, I enjoy staying active through soccer, both indoors and outdoors, and I’m also an avid music enjoyer. In my free time, I love exploring how emerging technologies can contribute to a safer, more transparent digital landscape for everyone.
